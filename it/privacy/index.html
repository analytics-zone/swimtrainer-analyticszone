Privacy Policy

Your swimming data is yours. The app is designed to work locally, without tracking, and you decide what you share.

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

At Swim Trainer, your privacy is not an option; it is the foundation of everything we build. We do not sell personal data and collect only the minimum necessary to provide the service.

Key Points

  • Local-first: By default, training data lives only on your device.
  • No tracking: We do not use third-party analytics SDKs or advertising tracking.
  • Coach Control: Collaboration is optional. You decide whether to share your entire history or just new workouts.
  • Easy unlinking: You can stop sharing data with your coach at any time.
  • Export and Deletion: Export your data or completely delete your account and data whenever you want.

1. Introduction

Swim Trainer ("we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our application and website.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with a personalized and synchronized experience across devices, we collect certain types of information:

2.1 User Accounts and Authentication

You can register and log in to Swim Trainer using email or third-party providers (Google and Apple). We receive the basic profile information necessary to manage your account.

2.2 Workout Data

We store the data for the workouts you create or perform (distances, times, types of sets, etc.). This data is securely synchronized with our servers only if you use collaboration features or cloud synchronization.

2.3 Integration with HealthKit

With your permission, Swim Trainer reads and writes workout data to Apple HealthKit. This integration is used exclusively to count your swim workouts in your general health metrics.

3. How We Use Data

We use the information to provide the service, synchronize your workouts across devices, offer you performance analysis, and provide important service communications.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted providers for backend hosting and authentication services (such as Google Firebase or Supabase) or when required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data. You can revoke access permissions to HealthKit at any time from the "Health" app on your iPhone.

6. Contact

You can contact us via email at analyticszone@onmedic.org.
