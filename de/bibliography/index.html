Bibliography and training references

Swim Trainer uses established swim training concepts and workload models, presented for practical coaching use.

The site content and product language are based on widely used concepts in competitive swimming and endurance training: critical swim speed, training zones, session load, efficiency metrics, and longitudinal athlete monitoring.

Reference areas

  • Critical Swim Speed (CSS) and pace-zone prescription for swimmers.
  • SWOLF, stroke rate, stroke count, and efficiency analysis from wearable swim sessions.
  • Training Stress Score style load tracking and performance management chart concepts.
  • Coach-athlete feedback workflows for reviewing sessions, comments, and exported summaries.

The app is a training and planning tool, not a medical device. Training decisions should still be adapted to the athlete, season phase, health status, and coach judgement.
